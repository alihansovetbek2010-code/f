    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about admission, UNT, documents, and universities

    What are the admission requirements for universities in Kazakhstan?

    +

    Admission requirements vary by university and program, but generally include:

    • UNT (Unified National Test) certificate
    • High school diploma
    • Medical certificate
    • Identity document
    • Photos

    Some programs may require additional entrance exams or interviews.

    What is UNT and when is it conducted?

    +

    UNT (Unified National Test) is a standardized test required for admission to most universities in Kazakhstan. It is typically conducted in June each year. Registration usually opens in March-April. The test covers subjects based on your chosen field of study.

    What documents do I need to prepare for admission?

    +

    Required documents typically include:

    • UNT certificate (original)
    • High school diploma (original and copy)
    • Medical certificate (form 086-U)
    • Identity document (ID card or passport)
    • 4 photos (3x4 cm)
    • Application form

    Some universities may require additional documents, so check with your chosen institution.

    How can I get a scholarship or grant?

    +

    There are several types of scholarships available:

    • State Educational Grants: Awarded based on UNT scores
    • University Scholarships: Merit-based scholarships offered by individual universities
    • Need-based Financial Aid: For students with financial difficulties
    • International Scholarships: For international students

    Check with your university's financial aid office for specific requirements and deadlines.

    What is the difference between public and private universities?

    +

    Public Universities: Funded by the state, generally lower tuition fees, more traditional programs, state grants available.

    Private Universities: Privately funded, may have higher tuition fees, often more specialized programs, modern facilities, fewer state grants but may offer their own scholarships.

    Can I apply to multiple universities?

    +

    Yes, you can apply to multiple universities and programs. However, each application may require separate documents and fees. Make sure to check deadlines for each institution.

    What subjects are tested in UNT?

    +

    UNT typically includes:

    • Kazakh/Russian language (mandatory)
    • History of Kazakhstan (mandatory)
    • Mathematics (mandatory)
    • Two profile subjects based on your field of study

    Do universities provide accommodation?

    +

    Most universities offer dormitory accommodation for students, especially those from other cities. Availability and conditions vary by university. Some universities have modern dormitories with various amenities, while others may have more basic facilities. Contact the university's accommodation office for details.
